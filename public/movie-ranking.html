Abdullah's Top 3 TV Shows

My Top 3:-

1. Breaking Bad

Wow. Simply wow. Best show of all time. Story, acting and development were all top notch. Has some of the best arcs in TV.

2. Better Call Saul

Just as great as it's sequel. Incredible acting from all actors. Especially loved the dynamics between the Salamancas and Nacho.

3. Peaky Blinders

Tommy Shelby. I mean, come on. Need I say more?
